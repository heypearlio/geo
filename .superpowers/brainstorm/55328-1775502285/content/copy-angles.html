Which angle feels most "you"? Each one speaks to the desire — not the situation.

Pick the hook that lands

A

The Curiosity Hook

"Curious What Your Home Would Actually Put in Your Pocket?"

Sub: Cash offer. Traditional sale. As-is. Run every scenario in 90 seconds — before you talk to anyone.

Tone: low-pressure, smart, no commitment. Best for cold/warm traffic who haven't decided yet.

B

The Control Hook

"Know Your Number Before You Make a Move."

Sub: See what you'd actually walk away with — across every selling option. No agent. No pressure. Just your numbers.

Tone: empowering, in-control, no-BS. Best for sellers who've been burned or feel out of the loop.

C

The Options Hook

"You Have More Options Than You've Been Told."

Sub: Most sellers never see the full picture. Cash offer, traditional list, as-is — run all three and see which one wins for you.

Tone: revelatory, slightly conspiratorial in a good way. Best for sellers who feel they only have one path.

D

The Net Proceeds Hook

"What Would You Actually Walk Away With?"

Sub: List price is not your number. See your real net — after fees, repairs, carrying costs — across every selling scenario.

Tone: specific, financially-savvy. Best for sellers who've thought about it but haven't seen real math.
